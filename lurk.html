ABOUT

Summary

This was the second project of my first year of university, where we were tasked with creating a point-and-click adventure game in Processing. Our game won the tournament of the best game created by students on that specific project.

Details

Tools: Processing

Date: November-December 2019

Development Time: 2 Weeks

Team: Artist: Finn Tran and Donislav Todorov Designers : Paul-Bogdan Grigore and Yonah Mercilia Engineer: Bram Reuling

Quick Summary

Lurk is a point and click interactive story, in which you play as Officer Jones, a cop which is tasked to investigate an apartment block in New York. Can you solve the murder case?
