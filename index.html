ABOUT ME

In the present day, I'm following the design role of Creative Media and Game Technologies at Saxion, in the Netherlands and I'm trying my best to create fun to play and easy to use applications.

I started blocking out environments around 2016 but back then I was focusing more on the looks and the composition of the shots rather on the gameplay aspect. That period is also when I started learning Unreal Engine 4.

I became more and more interested in game development in high school that is when I started "playing" with editors while creating small games. The high school period gave me a ton of free time in which I could play a lot of games which I could analyze and form opinions on how things were done. Later I've realised that I like being on the other side of games since I love watching people enjoy the experiences that I've created but also listening to their feedback in order to get better at doing it.

Outside of games I usually like taking pictures of whatever I find most interesting while going somewhere with my friends since I don't really being left alone.
